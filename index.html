WHO AM I?...

JOEL KIMAKWA.

An aspiring Footballer

Joel Kimakwa is from Kenya and dreams of being a professional football player. He started playing when he was 10 years old in primary school. His coach trained him to be good at attacking positions like winger, striker, and attacking midfielder. Joel loved football even more after that. In high school, he faced many challenges, but he didn't give up on his dream.
Finally, in Form 2, he got the chance to play for the school team because of his hard work and dedication. After high school, Joel still wanted to pursue football. He got selected for a fourth division team after playing for a subcounty team. Being scouted by Afc Leopards youth team academy was amazing for Joel because it brought him closer to his dream of being a pro footballer.
